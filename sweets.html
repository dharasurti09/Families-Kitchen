Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Khoya
  • Flour
  • Sugar syrup
  • Cardamom
  • Ghee

Steps:

  1. Mix khoya and flour into dough.
  2. Shape balls and deep fry.
  3. Soak in warm sugar syrup.
