Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Steps:

  1. Soak rice, dal, and fenugreek seeds for 6 hours.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter and ferment overnight.
  3. Make potato masala separately.
  4. Spread batter on hot tawa, drizzle oil, and stuff with masala.
  5. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
