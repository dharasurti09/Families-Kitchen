Breakfast: Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Potato filling

Steps:

  1. Soak rice and dal, then grind to a batter.
  2. Ferment overnight.
  3. Spread on tawa, add filling, cook until golden.

Vegetarian: Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Cook tomatoes with spices.
  2. Blend and strain.
  3. Add paneer and cream, simmer.
