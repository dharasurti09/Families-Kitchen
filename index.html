Featured Recipe: Chole Bhature

Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas
  • Flour
  • Spices
  • Oil
  • Onion and tomato gravy

Steps:

  1. Soak and boil chickpeas.
  2. Prepare spicy onion-tomato gravy.
  3. Mix and fry the bhature.
  4. Serve hot with chole.
