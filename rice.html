Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Mint and coriander

Steps:

  1. Cook vegetables with spices and yogurt.
  2. Layer with cooked rice and herbs.
  3. Steam until aromatic and fluffy.
