Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Potato filling

Steps:

  1. Soak rice and dal, then grind to a batter.
  2. Ferment overnight.
  3. Spread on tawa, add filling, cook until golden.
