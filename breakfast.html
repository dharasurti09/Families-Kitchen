Idli

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Soak rice and dal separately for 6 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter and ferment overnight.

Aloo Paratha

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • Green chili, coriander, spices

Steps:

  1. Prepare dough and potato filling.
  2. Stuff the dough balls and roll them flat.
  3. Cook on tawa with ghee until golden brown.

Poha

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • Mustard seeds, curry leaves

Steps:

  1. Wash and drain poha.
  2. Tempering with mustard, onion, and spices.
  3. Mix poha and cook for 5 minutes.
