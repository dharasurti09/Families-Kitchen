Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken
  • Onions, tomatoes
  • Spices
  • Oil
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices.
  2. Add chicken and cook thoroughly.
  3. Add water and simmer until done.
