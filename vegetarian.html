Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Cook tomatoes with spices.
  2. Blend and strain.
  3. Add paneer and cream, simmer.
